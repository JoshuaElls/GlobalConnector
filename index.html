Who We Are

The Specialist Your Cable & Connector Budget Has Been Waiting For

Global Interconnect, Inc. (Gii) is a US-Asia based engineering, supply chain, and manufacturing firm serving medical device OEMs across a broad range of capabilities. What We Build

Vertically Integrated Cable & Connector Manufacturing

Every capability under one roof — from connector molding and metal stamping to cable assembly and final test. Vertical integration is the engine behind Gii's cost-down results.

Cable Assembly Manufacturing

Complete cable assembly production from wire cutting through final electrical test — 100% tested on every unit. High-flex, shielded, coaxial, and multi-conductor options, all built to eliminate unnecessary cost at every step.

Custom Connector Molding

In-house injection molding of connector housings and overmolds eliminates outside vendor margins entirely. We control material selection, tooling, and production — giving you better connectors at a lower cost per unit.

Coating & Surface Treatment

In-house lubricity, biocompatible, and electrical performance coatings for cables and connectors — applied with precision and validated for your specific device requirements, single-use or reusable.

Subassembly & Integration

Full cable-to-connector subassembly and device integration services — handling the complex build steps between your cable and connector components so your team doesn't have to.

Metal Stamping & Contact Fabrication

In-house stamping of connector contacts, shields, and pins removes a critical cost layer from your supply chain. We hold tight tolerances at high volume — no outside fabricator markup.

The Gii Cost-Down Methodology

How We Drive Down Your Cable & Connector Costs

Cost reduction in medical cables and connectors isn't about cutting corners — it's about engineering intelligence, supply chain control, and the manufacturing scale that only comes from deep specialization. Here's how Gii delivers it.

DFM Reviews That Actually Save Money

Our engineers review your connector and cable designs for manufacturability before a single part is made. Catching expensive design choices early — excess contacts, over-specified materials, unnecessary complexity — is where the biggest savings are found.

Vertical Integration Eliminates Margin Stacking

Molding, stamping, coating, assembly, and test — all in-house. Every time a component crosses a vendor boundary, a margin gets added. Gii's vertically integrated model removes those layers and puts the savings directly into your COGS.

US-Asia Manufacturing Model

Engineering leadership and quality oversight in the US. High-volume production in Asia. This model gives you access to 10,000+ skilled workers and 1.5M sq ft of manufacturing capacity — at a cost structure that domestic-only manufacturers simply cannot match.

Zero-Failure Quality — Non-Negotiable

Cost-down never means quality-down at Gii. FDA-registered, ISO 13485 certified, and 100% electrically tested on every assembly — our quality systems are built to support your regulatory submissions while protecting patients and physicians in the field.
