Who We Are

The Specialist Your Cable & Connector Budget Has Been Waiting For

Global Interconnect, Inc. (Gii) is a global engineering, supply chain, and manufacturing firm with operations across the US, Americas, and Asia — serving medical device OEMs across a broad range of capabilities. What We Build

The Right Manufacturer for Every Part of Your Program

Rather than locking you into a single facility, Gii draws on a carefully vetted network of manufacturing partners and locations — matching each element of your cable and connector program to the specialist best suited to deliver it, at the best possible quality and cost.

Cable Assembly Manufacturing

Complete cable assembly production from wire cutting through final electrical test — 100% tested on every unit. High-flex, shielded, coaxial, and multi-conductor options, all built to eliminate unnecessary cost at every step.

Custom Connector Design

Expensive off-the-shelf connectors like LEMO and ODU are often over-specified for what your device actually needs. Gii engineers purpose-built custom connectors that meet your exact performance requirements — at a fraction of the branded price.

Coating & Surface Treatment

In-house lubricity, biocompatible, and electrical performance coatings for cables and connectors — applied with precision and validated for your specific device requirements, single-use or reusable.

Subassembly & Integration

Full cable-to-connector subassembly and device integration services — handling the complex build steps between your cable and connector components so your team doesn't have to.

Design for Manufacturability (DFM)

Before production begins, our engineers review your cable and connector designs for hidden cost — excess contacts, over-specified materials, unnecessary complexity. DFM is where the biggest savings are found, and it's built into every Gii program from day one.

The Gii Cost-Down Methodology

How We Drive Down Your Cable & Connector Costs

Cost reduction in medical cables and connectors isn't about cutting corners — it's about engineering intelligence, supply chain control, and the manufacturing scale that only comes from deep specialization. Here's how Gii delivers it.

DFM Reviews That Actually Save Money

Our engineers review your connector and cable designs for manufacturability before a single part is made. Catching expensive design choices early — excess contacts, over-specified materials, unnecessary complexity — is where the biggest savings are found.

Custom Design Replaces Expensive Off-the-Shelf Connectors

Branded connectors like LEMO, ODU, and Fischer carry a significant price premium — often far more than your device's performance requirements actually demand. Gii's engineers design purpose-built custom connectors that deliver equivalent or better performance at a dramatically lower cost per unit. This is typically where OEMs find their largest single source of cable and connector savings.

Flexible Global Manufacturing — Including Nearshore

Engineering leadership in the US. Nearshore manufacturing in Cartago, Costa Rica for programs that need US time zone alignment, fast lead times, and no China tariff exposure. High-volume production in Asia when scale demands it. You choose the model that fits your program.

Zero-Failure Quality — Non-Negotiable

Cost-down never means quality-down at Gii. FDA-registered, ISO 13485 certified, and 100% electrically tested on every assembly — our quality systems are built to support your regulatory submissions while protecting patients and physicians in the field.
